The Weather

Minnesota weather is extreme, unpredictable, and a bragging right for any Minnesotan. If you are not from the area, feel free to talk about the weather with any random stranger. It doesn't even have to be extreme weather. Simply reminiscing about the wiley mother nature will do.

Be prepared for sweltering heat upwards of 90 degrees and high humidity. Also be prepared for a 50 degree chill, rain and wind. It's really unpredictable. This said, we're hoping for a bright sunny 70 degree summer day.
