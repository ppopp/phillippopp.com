The Area

Nearby Towns

The farm is located next to several towns. Rice is a 10 minute drive to the west. Foley, home to Melanie's parents, lies 15 minutes to the south east. Both of these towns hold your standard grocery store, gas station, liquor store, McDonalds, local diner, and somewhere between 1000 and 3000 friendly locals. A larger town of >50,000 people can be found to the South West. The city of Saint Cloud contains several hotels, motels, malls, bowling allies, restaurants, etc.
